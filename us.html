Our Story

Founded to prove skincare and sustainability can coexist, EcoGlow crafts formulas built from responsibly sourced botanicals and scientifically proven actives. Our packaging is refill-first and recyclable.

Our Commitments

  • Refillable & biodegradable packaging
  • Ethically sourced ingredients
  • Transparent formulas & clinical testing

SCI Principle: Harmony in Diversity

We apply the Science of Creative Intelligence by designing harmonized formulations where botanical diversity meets scientific rigor. This principle shapes product design, packaging choices and customer education.
